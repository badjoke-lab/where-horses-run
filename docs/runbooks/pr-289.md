# PR-289 — Add country profiles 01–04

## Scope

This PR adds reviewed production profile-v2 records for the United Arab Emirates, South Korea, Turkey, and Morocco. It also adds the supporting official-source and principal-racecourse records used by the country-page runtime.

## Publication status

The four country pages become `profile_ready`, not `published`. Formal route, metadata, mobile, accessibility, and publication QA remains in PR #292.

Public timetable ceilings are A for the United Arab Emirates, South Korea, and Turkey. Morocco remains `pending` because the reviewed research did not establish a stable public timetable route.

## Principal navigation links

- United Arab Emirates: Meydan Racecourse
- South Korea: Seoul Racecourse
- Turkey: İstanbul Veliefendi Racecourse
- Morocco: Casablanca-Anfa Racecourse

These are representative country-page navigation links. The wider venue inventory remains recorded separately for later racecourse-page work.

Casablanca-Anfa remains `under_review`: the reviewed evidence supports the racecourse name as a navigation target, but does not yet establish its current active status, racing codes, course metrics, or timetable capability.
