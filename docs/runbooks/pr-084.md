# PR-084 — Japan official timetable source evidence pass

## Summary

PR-084 adds an evidence-only Japan timetable source review for JRA, NAR, and Banei. The purpose is to verify official source surfaces and document gaps before any more Japan timetable records are added or promoted.

## Files

- `docs/runbooks/japan-official-timetable-source-evidence.md`
- `scripts/check-japan-official-timetable-source-evidence.mjs`
- `docs/runbooks/pr-084.md`
- `package.json`

## Official evidence used

- JRA racecourse inventory: `https://japanracing.jp/en/racing/go_racing/jra_racecourses/`
- JRA official calendar: `https://japanracing.jp/en/racing/schedule/jra/2026.html`
- JRA Japanese calendar/date surface: `https://jra.jp/keiba/calendar2026/`
- NAR racecourse guide: `https://www2.keiba.go.jp/guide/`
- NAR current/date-specific race information: `https://www2.keiba.go.jp/KeibaWeb/TodayRaceInfo/TodayRaceInfoTop`
- NAR race-list URL surface: `https://www2.keiba.go.jp/KeibaWeb/TodayRaceInfo/RaceList`
- Banei schedule page: `https://www.banei-keiba.or.jp/race_schedule.php`
- Banei annual schedule announcement: `https://banei-keiba.or.jp/tp_detail.php?id=10288`
- Banei annual schedule PDF: `https://www.banei-keiba.or.jp/data/pdf/nenkan_kaisai_r8.pdf`

## What was verified

- JRA official evidence confirms the 10 JRA racecourses already present in repo inventory.
- JRA official evidence identifies the 2026 JRA calendar source and the Japanese calendar/date-page surface used by current JRA candidate records.
- Current repo JRA candidates are only four Tokyo/Kyoto records on 2026-05-30 and 2026-05-31.
- NAR official evidence confirms the NAR guide racecourse inventory and identifies date-specific race-list pages as the official source surface for meeting dates and `発走時刻`.
- PR-084 does not confirm a stable all-NAR annual/monthly calendar source covering every NAR racecourse.
- Banei official evidence confirms Obihiro as the venue and identifies the Banei schedule page as a meeting-date and first-race-time source surface.

## What remains unresolved

- JRA first-race-time extraction remains a manual-review gap.
- NAR exact first-race-time extraction remains unresolved; current generated NAR records store meeting-date evidence only.
- NAR stable all-racecourse calendar coverage is not confirmed.
- Banei existing generated times should be re-checked against the current official Banei schedule before any later public replacement.
- Active-window missing Japan racecourse/date gaps remain to be enumerated.

## What this does not do

PR-084 does not:

- add new country data;
- add new candidate records;
- promote timetable records;
- add live fetch runtime;
- add a source page parser;
- store raw official source bodies;
- add racecards, odds, results, payouts, predictions, or tips;
- replace a public overlay;
- claim Japan complete coverage;
- claim JRA complete coverage;
- claim NAR coverage is complete or broadly covered;
- claim Banei coverage is complete or broadly covered;
- add dependencies.
