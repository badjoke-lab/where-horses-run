# Calendar seasonal rollover

Status: active runbook  
Work ID: `WHR-CAL-OPS-V1`

## Trigger

Review a seasonal source when the next season is within 45 days, the source is older than 190 days, an active system has no meetings in the current 30-day window, or the official route materially changes.

## Procedure

1. Confirm official authority and source identity.
2. Record checked date and covered season or fixture window.
3. Reconfirm available timetable fields.
4. Keep Technical Rank and Public Ceiling unchanged unless separately reviewed.
5. Generate candidate data only; do not approve automatically.
6. Validate stale and empty-window behavior.
7. Keep previous public data only when the recorded fallback permits it.

## Empty window

An empty future window may mean off-season, delay, cancellation, or missing reviewed data. Confirm the cause. Do not create placeholder meetings.

## Completion

Rollover requires reviewed official evidence, fresh candidate scope, human promotion ownership, unchanged publication boundaries, bilingual rendering, and an identified rollback state.

Do not use unofficial mirrors, access bypasses, source-body publication, participant or betting expansion, automatic rank increases, or direct public writes.
