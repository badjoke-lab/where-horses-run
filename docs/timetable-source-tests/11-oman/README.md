# Oman timetable source test

## Result

- Status: Pending
- Technical capability: not assigned
- Tested season context: 2025-26
- Revalidation trigger: publication or opening of the 2026-27 season

No stable, country-wide official source was confirmed for obtaining
future Oman meetings together with Race 1..N and post times.

## Source findings

### Legacy Royal Horse Racing Club website

The former `rhrc.om` racing website is no longer usable as a
timetable source.

- the home URL redirects to a hosting-control page
- former ASP.NET calendar URLs return 404
- no surviving race-calendar or racecard structure was confirmed

### Oman Equestrian and Racing Federation

The current OERF website and WordPress REST API are reachable.

However:

- the public MEC collection contains 12 event records only
- those records date from 2021 to 2022
- they cover general equestrian events rather than the current
  national flat-racing programme
- searches found no OERF flat-racing posts dated 2025 or 2026
- the public results page exposed no timetable table, PDF link or
  post-time data

### Oman News Agency

Official ONA reporting confirms that the 2025-26 flat-racing season
operated at Al Rahba and that individual meetings commonly contained
eight races.

Most identified reports are retrospective result reports. They
confirm meeting dates, venue, race count and sometimes race labels or
distances only after the meeting has taken place.

An advance report for a major annual special meeting confirmed its
date and Al Rahba venue before the event. It did not provide a stable
national fixture list, complete Race 1..N programme or post times.
This isolated special-event announcement is therefore not adopted as
a country-wide rank C source.

## Rank decision

No technical rank is assigned.

A source suitable for normal timetable operation must provide at
least a repeatable future meeting date and venue. Higher ranks require
first/last times or complete Race 1..N post times.

The available sources do not yet provide that repeatable national
coverage.

## Revalidation

Recheck Oman when the 2026-27 season programme or opening meeting is
announced.

The recheck must determine whether an official source provides:

- a complete future meeting list
- meeting date and racecourse
- ordered Race 1..N programme
- first and last post times
- per-race post times
- race names, distances or other programme fields
- stable access suitable for automated refresh

## Publication boundary

Raw HTML, JSON, article bodies, participant names, results, weights,
betting information and payout information remain local-only and are
not published.
