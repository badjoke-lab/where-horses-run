# PR-236: Add meeting detail page contract

## Summary

This PR adds a public-safe meeting detail page contract for future A / A+ timetable routing.

## Contract

Meeting detail pages may show more than list pages, but only within the rank boundary.

A+ remains a limited programme summary and is not a full racecard.
