# PR-187: JRA normalized section records

## Current PR

This PR creates normalized JRA section records from the verified page-level rows.

It applies the PR-186 section split schema and the PR-184 B+ / A display policy.

## Added

- `data/generated/timetable/pr-187-jra-normalized-section-records.json`
  - Splits 2026-01-04 / 2026-01-10 / 2026-01-11 into separate Nakayama and Kyoto meeting records.
  - Adds `summary_records` for monthly calendar and day summaries.
  - Adds `detail_records` for A-capable meeting detail pages.
  - Keeps monthly calendar data separate from race-row detail data.

## Output boundary

- `summary_records` are for monthly calendar and day summary display.
- `detail_records` are for meeting detail pages.
- Monthly calendar must not expand race rows.
- B+ summary shape remains first_race_time plus last_race_time.
- A detail shape uses detail_page_path plus race times.
