# PR-183: Correct B+ and A display policy

## Current correction

PR-181 incorrectly allowed the plan to treat B+ as if it required complete race-by-race rows.

This PR restores the PR-132 source-of-truth rank boundary.

## Correct rank policy

- C = meeting_date plus racecourse confirmed, time unknown.
- B = first_race_time captured.
- B+ = first_race_time plus last_race_time captured.
- A = race-by-race schedule or racecard detail captured.

`last_race_time` is the final race start time, not meeting end time.

## Monthly display policy

Monthly display remains one row per meeting.

A-rank races are not expanded into the monthly page. They belong on meeting detail pages.

## Added

- `data/generated/timetable/pr-183-correct-bplus-a-display-policy.json`
  - Restores the PR-132 rank definitions.
  - Corrects B+ as first_race_time plus last_race_time only.
  - Defines A as race-by-race schedule or racecard detail.
  - Defines rank-specific monthly and day-page display contracts.
  - Requires A race rows to go to meeting detail pages, not monthly calendar rows.

## B+ display contract

B+ monthly row requires:

- meeting_date
- racecourse
- rank
- first_race_time
- last_race_time
- source_url
- verification_status

B+ may optionally show race_count if it is source-derived from verified rows.

B+ must not require race-by-race display.

## A display contract

A monthly row requires a meeting summary and a detail_page_path.

A race rows are shown only on meeting detail pages, not expanded in the monthly calendar.

## JRA implication

JRA calendar page rows can support A-capable detail data after section split, but the monthly calendar must still show only meeting summaries.

For B+, split meeting sections should derive first_race_time and last_race_time summaries.

For A, split meeting sections should route race-by-race rows to detail pages.

## Safety

- No JRA promotion in this PR.
- No calendar rows added in this PR.
- No rank change in this PR.
- Do not describe B+ as race-by-race schedule.
- Do not require complete race rows for B+.
- Do not expand A race rows in the monthly calendar.
- Keep monthly calendar one row per meeting for C, B, B+, and A.
